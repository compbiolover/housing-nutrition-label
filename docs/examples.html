Real Scenario: Andrew's ICF Duplex in Cooper-Young

A realistic build scenario: ICF duplex on a 0.12-acre lot in walkable Cooper-Young, Memphis. 900 sqft per unit, $330K total value. Solar panels, hurricane straps, hip roof, sealed roof deck. No passive house certification (keeping it affordable).

The house-hack math: $330K at 6.5% = ~$2,087/mo PITI. Rent unit 2 at $950/mo → effective payment $1,137/mo. With solar, your unit's energy is ~$10–15/mo. Less than renting a 1BR in the same neighborhood, while building equity in a tornado-proof, near-zero-energy building.
